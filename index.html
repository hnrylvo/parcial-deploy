The ergonomic design of the Shaver S101 fits perfectly in the hand, making it easy to handle and providing a secure grip. Its shape allows for effortless shaving and greater control during use.

Long Battery Life

This electric shaver is equipped with a high-capacity battery that offers up to 60 days of use on a single charge. It also features a fast-charging function for added convenience.

Shaver S101 includes a dual-motor system that provides additional power for superior performance with each shave. This ensures efficient shaving even in challenging areas.
